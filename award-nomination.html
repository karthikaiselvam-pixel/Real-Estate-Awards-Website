How It Works

Four Steps To Global Recognition

From submission to gala night — here is what happens after you complete your nomination.

1

Complete The Form

Fill in the official nomination form above. Include your project details, company information, and supporting evidence aligned to your chosen category.

2

Eligibility Review

Our awards secretariat verifies category fit, evidence quality and entry completeness before passing your submission to the independent jury.

3

Independent Scoring

A senior jury of global real estate executives, architects and analysts scores your entry against published criteria with full transparency.

4

Winners Announced

Finalists are notified ahead of the international gala. Winners receive global press coverage, a trophy, winner's seal and directory listing.

Nomination FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before completing your nomination.

Any property developer, real estate agency, broker, architect, interior designer, construction firm, smart-building company, property manager or proptech company from any country can submit a nomination.
There is no limit. Most organisations enter two to four categories. Multi-category entries may qualify for discounted entry fees. Submit a separate nomination for each category.
Include high-quality project photography, architectural drawings, performance data, sustainability credentials, customer testimonials and any relevant accreditations. Strong visual evidence significantly strengthens entries.
Entry fees vary by category and submission window. Early-bird entries qualify for reduced fees. Full pricing is displayed within the nomination form during the submission process.
Minor edits and additional supporting evidence can be uploaded before the entry close deadline. After the close date, submissions are locked for judging.
Reach the awards team at awards@goldentreeawards.com or via WhatsApp at +971 585862014. Our team responds to entry consultation requests within one business day.
