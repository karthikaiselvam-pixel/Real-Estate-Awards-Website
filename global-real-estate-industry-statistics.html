Market Data

Global Real Estate By the Numbers

The global real estate market represents one of the largest asset classes in the world, with total commercial real estate investment volumes exceeding $800 billion annually.

The Middle East real estate market is experiencing significant growth, driven by Dubai's continued emergence as a global property hub and major infrastructure investment across the GCC.

Asia Pacific remains the largest commercial real estate market by volume, with strong fundamentals across Singapore, Japan, Australia, India and Southeast Asia.

The residential real estate sector globally is valued at over $3.7 trillion in annual transaction volume, with luxury residential markets in Dubai, London, New York and Singapore leading international performance.

Sector Trends

Key Trends Shaping Global Real Estate

PropTech Investment: Global investment in property technology has exceeded $20 billion, transforming how real estate is bought, sold, managed and financed.

Sustainability: ESG-compliant real estate commands premium pricing, with green-certified buildings achieving higher occupancy and lower vacancy rates across major markets.

Smart Buildings: The smart building market is projected to reach $570 billion by 2030, driven by IoT integration and AI-powered building management systems.

Luxury Real Estate: Dubai's luxury market has recorded significant growth, with ultra-premium residential transactions consistently breaking regional price records.
