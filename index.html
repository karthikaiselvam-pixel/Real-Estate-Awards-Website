About The Programme

A Global Benchmark For Property & Real Estate Excellence

The World Real Estate Excellence Awards is an independent, jury-led recognition programme honouring exceptional work across every layer of the real estate value chain. From master developers shaping new cities to interior designers reimagining residential living, the awards celebrate measurable excellence in design quality, customer experience, sustainability and commercial performance.

Built around a transparent judging framework and dedicated regional categories, the programme is the most credible international platform for property developers, architects, agencies, brokers, construction companies and luxury real estate brands to gain global visibility and partnership-grade recognition.

Judging Process

From Nomination To Recognition

Transparent, independent and built on published criteria — here is how submissions become globally recognised winners.

1

Submit Nomination

Complete the online entry form, upload project files, design documents and supporting evidence aligned with your chosen category.

2

Eligibility Review

Our awards secretariat verifies category fit, evidence quality and entry completeness before forwarding to the judging panel.

3

Independent Scoring

An independent jury of senior global experts scores entries against published criteria, with weightings published in advance.

4

Winners Announced

Shortlisted finalists are notified, winners are revealed at the international gala, and recognition assets are issued for global use.
