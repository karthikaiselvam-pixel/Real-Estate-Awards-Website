What The Luxury Villa Awards Recognise

The Luxury Villa Awards honour individual luxury villas and luxury villa developments. The category set includes Luxury Villa Of The Year (single private villas at the top of their market), Luxury Waterfront Property Of The Year (oceanfront, beachfront, lakefront and riverfront villas), Luxury Mountain Property Of The Year (ski-in, alpine and mountain residence villas) and Luxury Villa Development Of The Year (multi-villa luxury communities).

The 2026 programme is open to luxury villa work across every region. Boutique architectural-led private villas, branded villa communities, resort villa developments, waterfront luxury estates and adjacent luxury villa product are all eligible. The programme is judged against published criteria specific to the villa segment.

Recognition matters in the luxury villa market because buyer decisions are intensely credibility-driven. Independent buyer research consistently shows that third-party recognition is the most trusted signal in HNW villa buyer decisions, particularly in cross-border purchases.

Why Luxury Villa Brands Compete For Recognition

Luxury villa recognition has direct commercial value. For developers selling luxury villas to international buyers, the award seal is a meaningful conversion lever in cross-border premium purchases. For architects delivering villa work, the award supports HNW client confidence and competition shortlist rates. For luxury villa operators, the recognition supports brand authority and guest experience signalling.

Past Luxury Villa Of The Year winners have consistently reported measurable commercial impact — stronger inbound buyer interest, accelerated cross-border sales velocity and stronger HNW referral flow in the period following the award.

How To Win A Luxury Villa Award

  • Lead with the villa's distinctive idea — design, location, scarcity, service.
  • Quantify outcomes — sales velocity, price achievement, buyer demographics.
  • Show the design — luxury villa categories particularly reward photography quality.
  • Address the buyer — who is this for and how do they experience it?
  • Submit independent luxury press and HNW concierge partnership references.
  • Address ecological integrity — landscape, biodiversity, environmental performance.
