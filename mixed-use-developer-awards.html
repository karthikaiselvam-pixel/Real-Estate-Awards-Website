What The Mixed Use Developer Awards Recognise

The Mixed Use Developer Awards and the parallel Master Developer Award honour organisations operating at the largest scale of property development — master plans, new cities, mixed-use destinations and integrated developments that combine residential, commercial, hospitality, retail and often civic uses into single coordinated programmes.

These are among the most consequential property assets in the world. The most successful mixed-use master developments shape urban experience, real estate value and city identity for decades. The category set is designed to recognise the strategic vision, multi-decade execution, infrastructure integration, community outcomes and design coherence that distinguishes outstanding master development.

The 2026 programme spans master developments across every region — from Saudi Arabia's giga-projects and the UAE's branded master plans to Singapore's integrated developments, India's mixed-use mega-projects, the UK's regeneration master plans, the USA's urban infill master plans, and Latin America's master-planned communities.

Who Should Enter

Entries are open to master developers and mixed-use specialists across every region and scale. Government-backed master developers, sovereign-aligned development platforms, listed master developers, private mixed-use specialists, regeneration developers and urban infill specialists all enter the programme.

  • Master Developers — Multi-decade master plans and new-city programmes
  • Mixed-Use Specialists — Integrated residential, commercial, hospitality destinations
  • Regeneration Developers — Urban infill, brownfield, large-scale regeneration
  • Sovereign / Government — Giga-projects, state-led master plans
  • Resort Master Developers — Tourism-led integrated destinations

Master Developer Of The Year, the headline category, weights multi-decade vision, infrastructure integration, community outcomes, design coherence and verified delivery against masterplan most heavily. The category is judged on the totality of the master-development programme rather than any single building or community within it.

Why Master Developers Compete For Recognition

Master developer recognition has uniquely high commercial value. For master developers raising capital for multi-decade programmes, independent global recognition functions as institutional-grade credibility with global lenders, sovereign LPs and equity partners. The Master Developer Of The Year award has been explicitly cited in capital-raising materials by several past winners.

For master developers competing for joint-venture, partnership and giga-project mandates, recognition is a meaningful tender-stage differentiator. For master developers selling residential and commercial product within their master plans, the award seal supports cross-selling of every unit type sold within the destination.

Talent is the third driver. Master development is among the most senior and complex property work in the industry, and award-winning master developers consistently report stronger inbound recruitment of senior development, design, infrastructure and commercial talent.

How To Win A Master Developer Award

Strong master developer entries are evidence-led at scale. They lead with the masterplan's strategic vision, follow with quantified outcomes across the programme (units delivered, infrastructure completed, employment generated, value created, community outcomes achieved), and present the design, infrastructure and operational layers of the masterplan in a structure the jury can score against published criteria.

  • Lead with the masterplan's defining idea — what it does that nothing else does.
  • Quantify multi-layer delivery — residential, commercial, hospitality, civic, infrastructure.
  • Show the masterplan — drawings, phasing, completion imagery, renderings of future phases.
  • Address community outcomes — employment, services, public realm, social infrastructure.
  • Submit lender, JV partner and sovereign-stakeholder references.

The Master Developer Jury

The Mixed Use Developer Awards jury includes senior master developers, urban planners, sovereign development executives, infrastructure specialists, real estate fund executives and academic experts on urbanism. Scoring weights masterplan vision, infrastructure integration, multi-decade delivery, design coherence, community outcomes and verified financial performance.

Submit Your Master Developer Awards Nomination

Nominations are submitted online via the official entry portal. Master developer categories include a longer-form supplementary entry covering masterplan phasing, infrastructure integration and multi-decade strategy. The awards team can be contacted directly for entry consultation.
