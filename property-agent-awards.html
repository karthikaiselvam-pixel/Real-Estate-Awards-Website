What The Property Agent Awards Recognise

The Property Agent Awards honour both individual property agents and property agencies. The category set is structured to recognise the dual nature of property sales — individual agent excellence and organisational platform excellence — across every segment of the property market.

The 2026 programme is open to property agents and agencies across every region. Independent agents, agency-employed agents, agency leaders, boutique luxury agencies, regional brokerage networks and global agency platforms all enter the programme.

Recognition matters in property sales because the buying experience is intensely personal and credibility-driven. Independent third-party recognition is one of the strongest credibility signals available to property agents and agencies competing for premium mandates and HNW buyer flow.

Who Should Enter

  • Individual Property Agents — Residential, commercial, luxury and mixed-segment
  • Property Agencies — Boutique, regional and global brokerage networks
  • Property Sales Teams — Specialist teams within larger agencies
  • Property Agency Leaders — Founders, managing directors and senior leaders
  • Property Agency Innovators — Technology-led agencies and platforms

Why Property Agents And Agencies Compete For Recognition

For individual property agents, recognition supports personal brand authority, mandate flow and HNW network access. Past Property Agent Of The Year winners have consistently reported stronger inbound mandate flow and higher conversion in HNW buyer conversations.

For property agencies, recognition supports brand authority, buyer trust, talent recruitment and competition in mandate-based channels. Property Agency Of The Year winners consistently report measurable commercial impact in the period following the award. The Property Agency Awards are followed closely by global luxury press, real estate publications and HNW concierge platforms.

How To Win A Property Agent Award

Strong agent entries lead with verified transaction performance, customer outcomes and brand authority. Strong agency entries lead with platform-level performance — agent count, transaction volume, market share, customer satisfaction and brand reach.

  • Quote verified transaction volume, GCI and market share.
  • Submit customer testimonials and verified satisfaction data.
  • Address market intelligence and segment specialisation.
  • Submit press coverage, partnership references and HNW network evidence.
  • Address platform innovation — technology, marketing, customer experience.

The Property Agent Jury

The Property Agent Awards jury includes senior brokerage executives, luxury market analysts, agency leaders, customer-experience specialists and academic experts on agent and agency performance.
