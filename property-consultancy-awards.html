About

Recognising Excellence in Property Consultancy

The Property Consultancy Award honours firms delivering outstanding advisory, research, valuation and transaction services to real estate clients worldwide.

From boutique advisory practices to multinational consultancy groups, this category celebrates firms shaping how investors, developers and occupiers make property decisions.

Eligible nominees include residential consultancies, commercial advisories, investment advisory firms, research houses and specialist property consultants operating in any region.
