About

Celebrating Excellence in Property Investment

The Property Investment Award recognises investment firms, funds and asset managers generating superior risk-adjusted returns while maintaining portfolio quality and ESG leadership.

Eligible nominees include private equity real estate firms, REITs, real estate funds, family offices, sovereign wealth funds and institutional investors with significant real estate portfolios.

The category evaluates investment performance, portfolio diversification, sustainability credentials, asset quality and value-creating transaction execution.
