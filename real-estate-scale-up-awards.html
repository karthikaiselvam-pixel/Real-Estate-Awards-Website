What The Real Estate Scale-Up Awards Recognise

The Real Estate Scale-Up Awards honour fast-growing property platforms — typically those that have moved decisively beyond start-up into scale-up over the past 18 months. The category set recognises growth velocity, operational maturity, market expansion, capital raised and verifiable customer outcomes. The headline category is Real Estate Scale-Up Of The Year, with sub-tracks for developer, agency, proptech and adjacent scale-up businesses.

Recognition is particularly valuable for scale-up businesses because the buyer, partner, investor and talent decisions they face are intensely credibility-driven. An independent global award functions as a credibility multiplier for scale-up developers raising capital, scale-up agencies competing with established networks and scale-up proptech businesses competing for enterprise mandates.

New Homes Award — Recognising The Best New Homes Developer

The New Homes Award sits alongside the scale-up track and recognises the year's best new homes developer. The category is judged on the quality of completed new homes product, sales velocity, customer satisfaction, design and delivery against published criteria. Sub-tracks cover Best New Homes Developer (residential), Best New Homes Project (single development), Best New Homes Marketing and Best New Homes Innovation.

Recognition has direct commercial value in the new homes market. Buyer research consistently shows that third-party recognition is a meaningful signal in new homes buyer decisions, particularly for buyers paying premium prices for branded, premium or design-led new homes.

Who Should Enter

  • Scale-up property developers — typically 1–5 years of accelerated growth
  • Scale-up real estate agencies — fast-growing brokerage networks and digital platforms
  • Scale-up proptech businesses — technology platforms reshaping property
  • New homes developers — primarily delivering brand-new residential product
  • Adjacent property scale-ups — operational, services or finance-led businesses

Why Scale-Up Recognition Matters Commercially

Scale-up businesses face an unusually concentrated credibility challenge. They compete with established incumbents who have decade-long track records, deep institutional relationships and well-known brand authority. An independent international award is one of the strongest credibility signals available to scale-up businesses competing in these conditions.

For scale-up developers raising institutional capital, the award functions as a credibility signal with LPs unfamiliar with the platform. For scale-up agencies competing with established networks, the seal converts at top-of-funnel. For scale-up proptech businesses competing for enterprise mandates, the seal supports tender-stage shortlist inclusion. Past scale-up winners consistently report measurable commercial impact in the period following the award.

How To Win A Scale-Up Or New Homes Award

Strong scale-up entries lead with growth evidence — quantified revenue growth, customer growth, geographic expansion, capital raised, market share gained. The jury weights verifiable growth metrics most heavily, with operational maturity, customer outcomes and team strength as supporting evidence. Generic 'we are growing fast' positioning language without supporting evidence scores poorly.

Strong new homes entries lead with the product — design quality, technical specification, customer experience, sales velocity, customer satisfaction. The jury weights completed-product quality and verifiable sales performance most heavily, with marketing innovation and customer-experience innovation as supporting evidence.

  • Quote verified growth — revenue, customers, geography, capital raised, market share.
  • Address operational maturity — team strength, governance, capital structure.
  • Quote customer outcomes — satisfaction, retention, advocacy, NPS where available.
  • Submit independent press, investor materials and customer references.
  • Show the work — product, platform, design, completed projects.
